About ICSP 2021

The International Conference on Security & Privacy (ICSP 2021) will take place at National Institute of Technology Jamshedpur, India, during November 16-17, 2021 in virtual/online mode. The ICSP 2021 aims to bring together researchers and experts in the field of security and privacy to share ideas, techniques, tools and experiences.

  • Acceptance Notification: September 1, 2021
  • Final Version Deadline: September 15, 2021
  • Registration Deadline: October 15, 2021
  • Conference Dates: November 16-17, 2021
